learn about its [history]({{ '/en/docs/history/' | relative_url }}) and [gameplay]({{ '/en/docs/thegame/' | relative_url }}), or play Retro-Fred, our modern multiplatform [port](/en/docs/retro-fred/ports/) of the original game.
